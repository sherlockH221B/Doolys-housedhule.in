    About us

    Dollys’ House English Medium School, Dhule - established in the year 1994, by founder and Principal Adv. Hemlata Jain, has had a prior experience of around 9 years of teaching at the Sophia Convent School, Mount Abu which is also her Alma Mater.

    Our Expertice in Dollys' House

    Wanting to do something for and with the children of Dhule city led to the foundation of the school we know today. The pre-primary section has completed 27 years of educational provision, primary with 15 years and the hope for middle school is in the pipeline. Dollys’ House helps students develop a motor base skillset, cognitive approach and integrated theme to each pupil.

    Online Optimistic approach During Pandemic

    Total holistic development of school and the students is always underway even with these unfortunate times of the pandemic where 70% of students have and will continue to gain experience online. Future plans for returning high quality hybrid learning sources by experienced and well trained teachers are underway.

    Our Belief

    Our firm belief is that every child, from all walks of life, irrespective of any worldly discrimination should have complete access to an excellent education for a suitable future. The school focuses on delivering quality education with english as not just a subject

    Skill Development

    The school focuses on delivering quality education with english as not just a subject but also a skill, however not disregarding the importance of the state and national languages. With the use of phonetics as a foundation of improvement.

    Extraordinary among the ordinary.

    The future plans for returning high quality hybrid learning sources by experienced and well trained teachers are underway. The schools’ motto remains “developing the Extraordinary among the ordinary.”
